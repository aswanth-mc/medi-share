How It Works

A calm, transparent flow from donation to safe collection.

Every step is designed around trust, verification, and simple access for donors, patients, and palliative care teams.

1

Donate Medicines

Donors submit usable medicines with expiry and quantity details through a simple form.

2

Units Verify Donations

Registered palliative units check safety, suitability, and availability before listing.

3

Users Request Medicines

Patients or caregivers find needed medicines and place requests with clear status updates.

4

Safe Collection Process

Approved requests move to coordinated pickup or handover through trusted local units.
